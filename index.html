About Team GrowUp

MAZE WITH US

Maze With Us is brought to you by Team GrowUp, our founders bring to you a unique and different approach towards the lives of girls everywhere.

Our aim is to spread issues through passion and start action. Join Team GrowUp to bring awareness about the challenges girls face everyday.

Be prepared to experience the reality that many girls have to face. Play our game and we hope you enjoy it!

Game

Info

Maze With Us is a fun and interactive game that follows the life of a girl, Alexa. Each level of the maze brings forth new challenges in the lives of girls. By successfully completing each level, Alexa will slightly grow up. This game functions to bring awareness to everyone about the challenges girls have to face.
