Importance:

MAZE WITH US reaches a wide scope of various themes and topics. These themes are often faced in the daily lives of girls around the world. If you would like to learn more about these issues please visit the following websites on the right. One of the reasons we decided to create this was because most of the targeted games for girls put an emphasis on cooking, makeup, and dressing up.
